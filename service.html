Waste collection technology and waste management

With our cutting-edge technology, we offer pick-ups of recyclable waste with our application and contact, we also offer waste management advice to industries.

Partnerships

We offer preexisting waste collectors with branded safety equipment and iOT scales. we also are open to partnering with preexisting dumps for storage, becoming a partner, and earning more.

Green iOT and “Acre currency”

With the help of our Internet of Things (iOT) which provides proof of good environmental and ethical participation for every stakeholder up and down the collection and recycling supply chain which can be monitored, incentivized, and ultimately trusted. Acre Cycle creates a reward system for an eco-friendly activity performed which is simply giving us your recyclable wastes through our iOT, we reward your efforts with eco points which can be converted to” Acre credits” which is backed up by blockchain technology and can be monetized.

Farm waste management

We offer all types of livestock agricultural businesses that produce a variety of waste a standard package for recycling their waste with acre cycle. Through our mission to enhance “food security,” we offer farmers a way of enjoying discounted rates in acquiring biofertilizers by giving us their livestock waste.

Child Education support foundation

We don’t only reduce carbon footprint and create a healthier environment we give back to society by investing in the future of Africa, through our child sponsorship foundation to enable customers to donate their incentives to support the education of underprivileged children.

Awareness creation

We create advocacy and educative platforms to enlighten the citizens about their big role in shaping and maintaining a smart and sustainable environment to improve the quality of life today and secure a green and pollution-free environment where all and our future generation can thrive
