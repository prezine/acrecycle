Sustainable waste management solutions

We recognize the importance of proper waste management in safeguarding our environment and public health. With this in mind, our efforts are focused on improving waste management practices, reducing waste generation, promoting the recycling and reuse of materials, and supporting the development of sustainable waste disposal infrastructure.

- Uchendu Prince, CEO

MISSION

Our mission at Acre Cycle is to provide sustainable waste management solutions and reduce the improper dumping of recyclable materials in Africa. We aim to achieve this by advocating for responsible waste disposal practices, educating citizens on the benefits of recycling, and promoting social responsibility for a cleaner and healthier environment.

VISION

Our vision at Acre Cycle is to become a leading eco-tech and agro-tech company in Nigeria and Africa, driving sustainable development through innovative waste management and agricultural practices. By leveraging cutting-edge technologies and collaborating with key stakeholders, we aim to create a circular economy that maximizes the value of waste materials while promoting food security, environmental sustainability, and economic growth.
